Solar Technologies

Solar technologies can harness this energy for a variety of uses, including generating electricity, providing light or a comfortable interior environment, and heating water for domestic, commercial, or industrial use.

this is a placeholder for an image

There are three main ways
to harness Solar Energy

Photovoltaics generate electricity directly from sunlight via an electronic process.

Solar Heating & Cooling use the heat generated by the sun.

Concentrating Solar Power use mirrors to concentrate the sun's energy.

Solar energy is a very flexible energy technology: it can be built as distributed generation (located at or near the point of use) or as a central-station, utility-scale solar power plant (similar to traditional power plants).
